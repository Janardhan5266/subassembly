SubAssembly is a project by Janardhan, created as a tribute to Schneider Electric Hyderabad. It helps streamline production updates by automating the process of sharing sub-assembly counts, shift-wise data, and line issues directly to WhatsApp.
The goal is to reduce manual work, save time, and improve accuracy in production reporting. By eliminating the need for manual WhatsApp updates, SubAssembly ensures a smooth and efficient workflow for the mobile pole line.

✔ Automated Sub-Assembly Reporting

Our system streamlines production reporting by automatically sending sub-assembly updates to WhatsApp groups. This ensures that supervisors and teams stay informed without the need for manual messaging, improving efficiency and reducing errors.

✔ Line Issue & Breakdown Reporting

Instantly report assembly line issues and machine breakdowns in real-time. By logging problems directly through the platform, maintenance teams can take immediate action, minimizing downtime and ensuring smooth operations.

✔ Part Waiting Alerts

Operators can notify supervisors about missing or delayed parts, preventing production stoppages. The system also allows tracking of part waiting duration, helping teams prioritize and manage resources effectively.

✔ Shift-Based Auto Updates

The platform categorizes production data based on shifts, ensuring that reports are clear and well-organized. It automatically detects the active shift, reducing manual input and preventing errors in logging shift-based production details.

✔ Cloud Storage & Database Integration

All reports and production data are securely stored in a cloud-based database. This allows easy access to historical records, ensuring that past production details can be retrieved whenever needed for analysis or audits.

✔ Customizable Workflows

SubAssembly is designed to adapt to your production needs. Whether it's modifying reporting formats, integrating new features, or adjusting automation settings, the system can be customized to enhance workflow efficiency.
